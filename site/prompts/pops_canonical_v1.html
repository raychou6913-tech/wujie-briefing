POP MART blind box flat illustration — a single small Ghibli-style sprite creature called "PEACH POPS", centered hero portrait on a solid wine-purple background (#1A1428). Aspect ratio 1:1.

═══ POPS DESIGN DNA (lock these properties exactly — every future POPS will share this) ═══

SHAPE: round egg-shaped body, slightly taller than wide (height : width ≈ 1.15 : 1). Soft rounded silhouette, no sharp corners. Body fills about 70% of the canvas height (large hero portrait, not tiny).

PROPORTIONS: the entire creature is one rounded body — NO neck, NO arms, NO ears. Head and body merge into one egg shape. At the bottom, two tiny stub feet poke out (about 8% of body height), close together.

EYES: two large round shiny black pupils, positioned in the upper-middle of the body (eye line at about 45% from top). Each eye has a small white catchlight reflection in the upper-left corner of the pupil. Eye spacing: about 1 eye-width apart. Eye size: each eye about 18% of body width.

MOUTH: tiny "u"-shaped smile, drawn in the same black outline weight as the body line, positioned centered below and between the two eyes, about 12% of body width. Closed smile, no teeth, no tongue.

OUTLINE: 3-4px thick HAND-DRAWN black outline around the entire body and around each feature (eyes, mouth, feet). Lines have subtle wobble / imperfect quality — NOT perfect vector. Outline is a warm dark color, near-black but slightly softer than pure black.

COLOR: solid saturated peach orange (#FF8B5C). FLAT 2-tone shading only — main color + ONE darker peach shade (#E66E45) applied as a soft shadow on the bottom 30% of the body and underneath the eyes/mouth area. NO gradients, NO highlights other than the eye catchlights, NO anime gloss, NO photorealistic rendering.

FEET: two small stub feet at the bottom, drawn in the darker peach shadow color (#E66E45), each foot about 12% of body width, close together, slightly visible from front.

POSE: standing facing viewer, body very slightly tilted backward (head looking up about 5°), shy but content expression.

═══ BACKGROUND ═══

Solid wine-purple #1A1428, completely flat, NO scenery, NO gradient, NO texture. Just one solid color filling the entire canvas. The POPS sits centered with generous padding (about 15% margin on all sides).

═══ DO NOT ═══

— NO arms, NO hands, NO ears, NO antenna, NO horns, NO hat, NO accessories
— NO sharp corners or angular shapes
— NO gradients in body color
— NO photorealistic rendering or 3D look
— NO white background, NO gradient background
— NO multiple POPS — only ONE

═══ STYLE REFERENCES ═══

POP MART blind box × Studio Ghibli soot sprites (Susuwatari) × 80s Japanese sticker book × DIMOO designer toy outline quality.

DOLL FEASIBILITY: this design must be moldable as a vinyl figure — single solid blob with two stub feet for stability, no thin protrusions, no transparent parts, no flowing elements.
